Case Studies

Behind every number is a strategy. Here's how I think through problems.

Challenge

The sales team had no structured outbound process. Reps were doing ad-hoc outreach with inconsistent messaging and low reply rates.

Approach

Built a 3-step multi-channel sequence: cold email day 1, LinkedIn connection day 3, follow-up call day 7. Wrote new ICP-specific messaging for each persona.

Results

Within 30 days, booked 38 qualified meetings. Reply rate jumped from 2% to 11%. Pipeline value increased by 3x compared to the previous quarter.

38 Meetings in 30 Days 11% Reply Rate 3x Pipeline Growth

Challenge

Newly formed SDR team of 4 reps with no playbook, no benchmarks, and inconsistent output. Needed a system for tracking and improving performance.

Approach

Created a weekly KPI dashboard, built sequence templates in the CRM, and ran bi-weekly 1-on-1 coaching sessions focused on call recordings and messaging refinement.

Results

Team output increased by 60% in 90 days. All 4 reps hit quota by month 3. Onboarding time for new SDRs was cut from 6 weeks to 3 weeks.

60% Output Increase 100% Quota Attainment 3-Week Onboarding

Challenge

Pre-revenue SaaS startup with a great product but no go-to-market motion. Founders were doing sales themselves with zero structured outbound.

Approach

Conducted ICP research, built a target account list of 500 companies, set up Apollo.io for sequencing, wrote a 5-email cold email sequence, and trained the founding team.

Results

Generated 22 discovery calls in the first 6 weeks. 5 converted to paid pilots. Helped the startup close their first $50K ARR through outbound alone.

22 Discovery Calls $50K ARR Pipeline 6 Weeks
